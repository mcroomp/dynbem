## Notes

- The 4-ft rotor data spans the same λ₁/λ₂ range as the 6-ft data, confirming the paper's
  conclusion that rotor diameter has no significant effect on the λ₁ vs λ₂ curve.
- Dynamic twist corrections for this rotor:
  θ₀.₇₅R = 0.960θ_root at 1600 rpm
  θ₀.₇₅R = 0.965θ_root at 1200 rpm
